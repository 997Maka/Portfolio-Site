About

My Story

I come from an academic background in public affairs and communications but have always expressed my work through design. Having worked with different audiences and stakeholders at all levels, I love to use quality design to simplify complex information and to tell stories with a purpose.


I see the world through shapes, colours, and patterns and this inspires me to approach all problems from a creative angle. I believe that quality design can really bring out the beauty in information. I obsess over perfection in details, but never lose track of the big picture and objectives. So, when I decided to transition to UX design, it all felt natural to me.


Aside from design and work, I am a coffee addict, history buff, avid gamer, foodie, have an unhealthy obsession with collecting mugs, and overall, a very curious person. One day I will be immersed in researching the Mongolian conquest, the next, deep in discussion about time relativity in Interstellar, then playing video games until 3 am.
